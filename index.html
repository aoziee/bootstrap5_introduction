Sanrio

Sanrio is a Japanese company renowned for creating adorable characters that have captured hearts worldwide. Established in 1960, it's famous for icons like Hello Kitty, My Melody, Little Twin Stars, and many others. Sanrio characters span various merchandise, from stationery to toys, clothing, accessories, and even themed cafes and amusement parks. With their timeless charm and widespread appeal, Sanrio characters continue to bring joy to people of all ages across the globe.

Cinnamoroll

Cinnamoroll is one of the beloved characters created by Sanrio. He's an adorable white puppy with long ears that enable him to fly. Originally introduced in 2001, Cinnamoroll quickly became a favorite among fans for his sweet demeanor and charming personality. He's often depicted surrounded by fluffy clouds or engaging in fun adventures with his friends in the magical world of Cafe Cinnamon. Cinnamoroll's design and story evoke a sense of innocence and whimsy, making him a cherished character among both children and adults.

My Melody

My Melody is another cherished character from Sanrio, beloved for her sweet and gentle nature. She's a cute little rabbit known for her signature pink hood, which she always wears, adorned with a cute little bow. My Melody debuted in 1975 and has since captured the hearts of fans worldwide with her endearing personality and charming adventures. Often depicted surrounded by flowers or playing with her friends, My Melody embodies kindness and warmth. Her timeless appeal makes her a beloved icon in the world of Sanrio, appealing to both children and adults alike.

Support Us

By backing Sanrio, you're not only celebrating creativity and imagination but also supporting a company that has brought joy to millions worldwide for decades.
